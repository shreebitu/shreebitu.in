Why Macro Gamer?

Are you tired of doing the same repetitive clicks and keystrokes in your favorite games or daily workflow? Macro Gamer is a powerful, lightweight automation tool for Windows designed to bridge the gap between simple recorders and complex scripting engines. Unlike generic tools, it offers the precision required for high-stakes gaming while remaining simple enough for anyone to use. Whether you're looking to dominate a leaderboard or automate a tedious data-entry task, Macro Gamer gives you the absolute control you need.

Record your mouse clicks and keyboard presses in real-time as you perform them.

Custom Bindings

Assign complex macros to any key or mouse button for instant, one-tap execution.

Infinite Looping

Troubleshooting & FAQ

Macros not saving?

By default, macros are saved to the installation directory: /Program Files (x86)/MacroGamer. This requires administrative Write Permissions which modern Windows accounts often restrict.

01
Recommended Fix

Create and save your MacroGamer profiles in your Documents folder or on the Desktop.

02
Alternative Fix

Right-click the shortcut and choose Run as administrator. (Use only if Solution 1 fails).
